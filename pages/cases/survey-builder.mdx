# Customer Feedback Survey Builder

**Role:** PM • **Scope:** Discovery → MVP → Launch  
**Outcome:** Reduce time-to-insight by 60%, ↑ response rate by 22%

## Problem
Legacy survey tooling slowed customer insight cycles and fragmented data.

## Approach
- Lean discovery with 12 stakeholder interviews
- MVP builder with AI-generated question banks
- Auto-routing to CRM and data warehouse

## Results
- 60% faster setup, +22% response rate, ↓ cost per insight by 35%
