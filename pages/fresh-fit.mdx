# 🛠️ Fresh Fit Snacks Website Rebuild

A **7-day solo sprint** to rebuild a DTC snack brand’s underperforming site.

## Problem
- Fragmented brand voice weakened trust  
- Poor mobile UX drove high bounce rates  
- No feedback loop blocked customer validation  

## Solution
- ✅ Mobile-first redesign with Squarespace  
- ✅ AI-assisted copywriting + brand refresh  
- ✅ Feedback forms embedded into product + checkout  

## Outcome
- 🚀 Shipped site in < 7 days  
- 📉 43% bounce rate reduction  
- 📈 2.3× lift in mobile conversion  
- 🏪 Enabled expansion into 1,200+ retail doors
